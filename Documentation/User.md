## User
Upon following the Development set up, you can run ```.\dockerRun.ps1``` and access the application at https://localhost:7777.

When you access the application, you will be prompted to log in.
<img width="1898" height="912" alt="rothscope_1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cddcb756-cb08-456f-aca2-4a4afbdf45c9" />

After logging in, you will be brought to the client home page. From here, you can either select a current client or create a new one.
<img width="1903" height="905" alt="rothscope_2" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e3e1ffff-39af-4bfb-8aff-0379db4562e2" />

Suppose you want to create a client. So you click on the button, and then you are brought to this page wherein you would fill out your client's information.
<img width="1902" height="907" alt="rothscope_3" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/84dec327-d481-4d48-95cf-ebb7894a6b37" />

Going back to the client list page, we select Smokey Bear. We are then brought to Smokey's Scenario view page. From here, you can either slect an existing scenario or create a new one.
<img width="1918" height="906" alt="rothscope_4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/50ab41de-1b1b-491c-bea1-49b175f7c809" />

Suppose you want to create a new scenario. So you click on the button, and then you are brought to a page for naming your scenario.
<img width="1918" height="907" alt="rothscope_5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c9f7c587-7086-4c25-aa31-5540fad4b4ff" />

Upon naming and clicking "Create Scenario", you are brought to a page wherein you will fill out the assumptions of said scenario.
<img width="1902" height="907" alt="rothscope_6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d0669966-fef2-4207-90cd-682f7b5ee6dd" />

When you complete that, you will be brought to the assets page of your specific scenario.
<img width="1900" height="907" alt="rothscope_7" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/66e73aa0-6cdd-4cd0-a185-1064980cda5d" />

This is what it looks like when you create an asset.
<img width="1905" height="915" alt="rothscope_8" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d3014979-de81-46a6-8c06-011fd999d705" />

When you are all finished, you will be at the same point as if you had selected an existing scenario---the optimization screen. Here, you set different parameters to convert your assets.
<img width="1903" height="910" alt="rothscope_9" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e444ab65-7f97-48a3-b7b0-5aa7181212af" />
